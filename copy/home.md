## The Last 20% (why human-AI hybrid)

**Section header:** AI gets you 80% there. The last 20% is where it gets expensive.

AI can draft a report, a post, a proposal, a plan, fast. What it can't do on its own is match your voice, catch the details that matter, make the judgment calls only you can make, and package the result so it's actually ready to use.

That last 20%, the context, the correction, the polish, is where most people end up spending hours anyway. Sometimes it ends with "maybe I should have just done this myself."

SFTech is built around closing that gap. AI handles speed and leverage. A human handles judgment, quality control, and the final result. You explain the goal once. We handle the rest.

## How We Start

**Section header:** Five steps, starting free.

2. **AI Opportunity Map.** A short, prioritized list of what's worth doing, what isn't, and why.
3. **Choose your path.** Implement it yourself, bring in someone else, or have us execute it.
4. **Small pilot.** If we execute, we start with one narrow workflow, not a giant system.
5. **Review, improve, or stop.** We look at what worked, what didn't, and whether it's worth continuing.
