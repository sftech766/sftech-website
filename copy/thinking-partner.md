## What You Don't Have to Do Afterward

An AI Thinking Partner engagement never requires you to buy execution from us. Once you have your audit or opportunity map, you can:

1. Implement it yourself.
2. Bring in someone else to build it.
3. Ask us to execute it, if that's useful.

The first goal is finding the right work. Selling you delivery comes second, if at all.

## What We Deliver

### AI Workflow Audit
A structured review of your current workflows, tools, content processes, research processes, customer journey, and repeated tasks.

### AI Opportunity Map
A prioritized list of where AI or human-AI support could help, what should not be automated, what's low-risk to test first, and what could create the highest return.

### Workflow ROI Review
A simple way to compare possible projects by time saved, revenue potential, quality improvement, difficulty, risk, and implementation effort.

### Automation Readiness Review
A check of whether a workflow is actually ready for automation, or whether it first needs clearer inputs, SOPs, content templates, or decision rules.

### Implementation Thinking Partner
Ongoing support if you're implementing a workflow yourself or through someone else, but still want a second set of eyes on scope, quality, risk, and return.
